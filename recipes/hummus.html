Hummus

Description

Also spelled hommus or houmous, is a Middle Eastern dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. The standard garnish in the Middle East includes olive oil, a few whole chickpeas, parsley, and paprika.

Ingredients

Only 7 basic ingredients are needed:

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • 1/3 cup Tahini
  • 2 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • 5 tbsp Water
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt

Steps

To make great hummus, follow the steps below:

  1. Grab a blender
  2. Throw the ingredients in
  3. Process until smooth, adding water as necessary
